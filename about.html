Hi, I'm Winnie.

擁有資管背景且充滿熱情的產品設計師。我喜歡慢跑、爬山、做瑜伽，除了可以讓身心靈放鬆，還可以得到更多創意靈感。平時也愛看書、聽聽Podcast，吸取各方面的知識，得到自我成長的能量，也讓自己在UX這條路上眼界更開闊，更能傾聽不同的聲音。

A passionate product designer with an information management background. I like to do jogging, mountain climbing and yoga , so that refresh my mind, body, and help me get more creative ideas. I also like reading and listening podcast to learn new knowledge and improve myself. Keep an open mind to listen and do UX research with an empathy heart.

Why I decide to change career to UX?

原本專注在網頁及APP設計領域，就在某一個專案會議上被問到「為什麼你覺得這按鈕應該在這個位置？為什麼採用這個顏色？」當我回答不出來的時候，我發現這一切都是自己認為的好設計。因此，我開始google「什麼才是所謂的好設計？」

I focus on web and APP design until I were be asked the question at the project meeting."Why do you think this button put on this position? why do you choose this color on this design?" I found that is "I feel it is a good design." when I can't answer the question.Therefore, I begin to google "what is the good design" mean.

然而，在2016年我決定踏上UX這條路，參加了為期十週的實戰營，並在這十週裡組團隊一起完成了一個數位產品ShaRead並發表，我得到了所謂的「成就感」。因此，不僅是對於UI/UX領域，也對整個產品開發流程，有了更深入的了解。並且發現「剖析產品設計背後的真因」這件事，讓我重拾對設計的熱情與執著。

However, I decide to join the product design camp(Alphacamp) within ten weeks at 2016. I formed a team to build and create the
ShaRead project by a product designer, engineers and marketing. And, I can feel a sense of accomplishment after completing this project. Through this project, I realized how to fit UI/UX practices to the real project via collaboration and listening. Also, I found that "Analyze the real reason behind product design" let me have more passionate and perseverance about design.

為了挑戰自己，決定探索除了在數位產品之外，在物聯網產品，甚至實體產品上，有什麼不一樣UX挑戰。因此，我跟團隊一起完成了智慧佛珠、智慧水杯，智慧穿戴裝置，還有可撓式筆電及雙螢幕筆電等創新產品。因此，對於「物聯網/實體產品的UI/UX研究分析」及「科技新知的敏銳度」提升非常多，甚至增進了「製作簡報及提案能力」。除此之外，團隊也會針對產品的創意點子申請專利，因此也累積了非常多的「專利撰寫能力」。

In order to challenge myself, except the digital product, I decided to explore different UX field in IoT and physical products. I have been building innovative products with teams in Inventec such as Smart leap beads, smart tumbler, smart wearable device, flexible screen device and dual screen laptop etc. Hence, I learned how to do UI/UX research on physical/IoT products and improve the sense of technology awareness. Also, I apply multiple patents to prove and protect my invention. That definitely help my writing skills because the documents should be well-defined and internal reviewed.

"Run hard, be strong, think big." It's my favorite quote.

我認為跳出舒適圈，不一定是要在職涯上有什麼很大的突破，或是功成名就。而是在日常裡做了些微的改變，日積月累下來的習慣。像是原本給自己訂下「一個月看4本書」的目標，在達成後晉升為「一個月看6本書」。長久下來看會發現，其實已經完成了很多「自我突破」的事物，而且一點也不難。最後，期許自己能不斷地挑戰新事物及突破自我。

I believe step outside the comfort zone is not meaning you need to push yourself too hard or be famous in your career. You just have little change and become your habits in daily day. For example, your target is reading four books a month begin, and up to six books a month when you achieve the target. It's not difficult to completed more target for long-term. Finally, hoping to keep challenging new things and pushing myself constantly.
