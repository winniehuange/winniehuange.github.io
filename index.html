[Project Brief]

我想要做一個媒合的平台，讓喜愛閱讀又樂於分享書籍的人，分享自己家的書櫃，並且透過平台開租書店，已出租方式租給想要閱讀的人。會想要以租借的方式，是因為想要利用互惠的方式分享書籍，一方得到租金，一方得到書籍內容、書評及個人觀點等等，所以我也開始著手設計這個產品，並且取名為「ShaRead」！

I want to create a platform which can let people who like reading and enjoy sharing their bookshelf at home, and through platform to open bookstore so that people can rent books to someone who want to read books.
Because of using mutually beneficial way to share books, one get money, another get books content, comment and some personal view, I prefer to rent book model.
Therefore, I begain to design this product, and name it「ShaRead」.
